FAQ

What are the rules?

You are required to be a College/University student with a valid Student ID card. Also, you must not start coding before the hackathon starts.

What do I gain?

A lot of fun, meeting new people, recruiting to some of the best companies, experience making a project and collaborating, free T-shirt, free food and a lot more.

What if I don't have the required skills?

No need to worry about that, we will lauch pre-hackathon bootcamp and workshops before.

How much does it cost?

Registration is free of charge!

Do I need to bring anything?

Just your laptop, we'll take care of anything else.

What about food?

There will be lots of it. :)
